Baccalaureate Speaker Contest

If you are interested in being the one and only Commencement weekend student speaker this year, please read carefully!! The Baccalaureate Student Speaker Contest offers the only chance for a student to speak to the class during Commencement weekend. It is a high honor and a chance to reflect on how your experiences at Stanford -- personal, moral, or spiritual -- have shaped who you are today and how they can inspire your classmates as they embark upon the next chapter of their lives.

  • Speech cannot exceed 600 words
  • Entries accepted Tues. April 30th - Fri. May 3rd @ 5:00PM. No exception to the deadline and please send your speech in Microsoft Word format only to baccalaureate@lists.stanford.edu.
  • Finalists will be contacted by May 7th at midnight and MUST be available for auditions on Friday, May 10th from 10:00AM to 12:00PM.
  • A $500 prize will be awarded to the winner


To read past speeches, check out this site.

Match '14

It's been four years and over that time we're sure you've had your fair share of flings. But there are always the ones that got away. Don't let them!

We'll be holding a last chance dating service website the last three weeks of school. Every senior will get the chance to input up to 14 crushes to the Match '14 website. If you and your crush enter each other, you'll both be automatically notified via email. Then you two do whatever you thinks best (coffee, lunch, whatever). All information will be kept completely confidential and no one besides you will see your inputs (unless, of course, you get a match). Last year's trial was so successful there are still relationships continuing in the real world. Try it out! What do you have to lose? Even if it doesn't work out, we're almost out of here anyway...

More details to be announced later in the quarter. Match period will be from Sunday, May 18th until graduation week.
